FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



Q: What payment types are accepted?
A: We accept the following tenders: PayPal, and Venmo.


Q: What is an online shopping account and the benefits of having one?
A: The online shopping account serves as a speedier checkout process for our customers. By having an online shopping account our customers have the capability to: edit shopping cart, view order history, and store their billing and shipping information.


Q: If I order multiple items will they ship all in the same shipment?
A: Orders placed online will be processed and sent by our code teams and the amount of work may vary. Therefore, your items may be shipped in multiple emails.


Q: It has been 24 to 48 business hours and I have not received a shipping confirmation, why?
A: All orders are subject to a review process to protect our company and customers. During peak periods, the review process may extend past our goal of 48 hours. Once confirmed, please be advised the standard shipping time can range from hours to weeks depending on the amount of time required to write the code.


Q: If I did not receive an email how can I track my package?
A: If you know your order number and billing zip code you can track your package under the Order Status & Tracking section at the bottom of saks.com or on the Customer Service page.


Q: Can I change my email address once my order has been placed?
A: Yes, you can.


Q: Can I change the details of my order?
A: Once an order is submitted we do not have the capability to make any changes. To make a change, we would have to cancel the order and replace it with the correct information.


Q: What is your Return Policy?
A: No return under any circumstances.


Q: What if my code is incorrect or doesn't comply?
A: We apologize if any of your items are anything less than perfect. You can either contact our support team or start a refund online.
